Kreader - RSS reader

Enter your RSS feed in one of the below boxes and press Open. Your can chose to view the full RSS feed or only the headlines.

Examples:

The e-ink reddit and the same with only headlines
The Earth pics reddit and the same with only headlines
Daring Fireball RSS and the same with only headlines

You can make your own bookmarks by adding your RSS feed URL to http://localhost:5000/?url=

Like so http://localhost:5000/?url=http://RSS-DOMAIN.com/RSS-FEED
